YouTube Recommendation #5: The Otaku Take

The next channel that I would like to recommend is, in my opinion, the most underrated podcast that I have watched on YouTube called the Otaku Take.

They are a group of 6-8 otakus/weebs who love to geek out about anime, video games, and other media.

One particular member that I would personally recommend watching, however, is Hyper Shane, a retro game player and Twitch streamer with 132 subscribers.
